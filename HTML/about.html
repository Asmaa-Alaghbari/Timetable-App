About

This website is a Timetable App designed to help you organize and manage your daily schedule effectively. It provides a user-friendly interface where you can input and save your tasks, appointments, and notes for each day of the week. The app allows you to customize the timetable colors, generate random quotes for motivation, and change the background color of the page. Stay organized and productive with the Timetable App!
